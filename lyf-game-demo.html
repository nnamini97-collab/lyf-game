🔒 LYF Privacy Policy
✅ COPPA COMPLIANT

LYF is built for children. We never sell your child's data. We never show ads. We collect only what is strictly necessary to run the app — and parents are in full control.

EFFECTIVE DATE & OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy was last updated on January 1, 2026. It covers the LYF mobile application ("LYF," "the App") operated by LYF Learning Inc. ("we," "us," or "our").

LYF is an educational game designed for children ages 7–10. We are fully committed to compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506, and the FTC's implementing regulations at 16 C.F.R. Part 312.

1. VERIFIABLE PARENTAL CONSENT

Under COPPA, we are required to obtain verifiable parental consent before we collect any personal information from a child under 13. We do this through a two-step process:

  • Email verification: The parent's email address is collected at account creation. A verification email is sent before the account is activated.
  • Consent acknowledgment: Parents must actively check a box confirming they have read and consent to this Policy on behalf of their child.

We do not allow a child to access personalized features until parental consent is confirmed.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the App. Here is exactly what we collect:

  • Parent/guardian email address — used only for account access, consent verification, and safety notifications. Never shared with third parties for marketing.
  • Child's first name or nickname (parent-provided) — displayed in-app to personalize the experience. Never publicly visible.
  • Child's age range (e.g., "7–8") — used to calibrate content difficulty. Not an exact birthdate.
  • In-app progress and gameplay data — mission scores, skills unlocked, XP earned. Used to power the Parent Dashboard and the Adaptive AI Engine. Never sold.
  • Device type and OS version — anonymous technical data used solely for performance optimization and bug fixing.

We do NOT collect: precise GPS location, real photos or video of the child, voice recordings, or any financial information from children.

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Data we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Delivering the educational game experience to the child
  • Powering the Parent Dashboard with skill progress and insights
  • Personalizing learning difficulty through the Adaptive AI Engine
  • Sending parents optional weekly progress summaries (opt-in only)
  • Improving app stability and fixing technical issues

We do NOT use children's data for behavioral advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose outside the direct delivery of the App's educational service.

4. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell, rent, or trade children's personal information to any third party, ever.

We share data only with a strictly limited set of service providers who help us run the App under binding data protection agreements, including:

  • Cloud infrastructure provider (e.g., AWS or Google Cloud) — for secure data hosting only
  • Analytics provider — for anonymous, aggregated app performance data only (no child identifiers)

All service providers are contractually prohibited from using LYF data for their own purposes or for advertising.

5. PARENTAL RIGHTS UNDER COPPA

As the parent or legal guardian of a child using LYF, you have the following rights, which we will honor promptly:

  • Right to Review: You may request a complete copy of the personal information we have collected about your child at any time.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your child's personal information. Upon confirmed deletion, data is permanently and irreversibly removed within 30 days.
  • Right to Refuse: You may refuse further collection or use of your child's information by deleting the account. The App may cease to function without this data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us (see Section 8). Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of prior data use.
6. DATA SECURITY

We use industry-standard security measures to protect all data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls limiting who can view child data internally
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • No child data is stored locally on devices beyond the current session cache

In the event of a data breach affecting children's data, we will notify affected parents within 72 hours and report to the FTC as required by law.

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain child data only as long as the account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • All personally identifiable data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous, aggregated data (no child identifiers) may be retained for product improvement
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days
8. CONTACT US

For any privacy questions, to exercise parental rights, or to request data review or deletion, please contact our Privacy Officer directly:

We respond to all privacy requests within 5 business days. Deletion requests are confirmed within 10 business days.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we make material changes to how we handle children's data, we will notify parents by email before those changes take effect and will obtain renewed consent if required by COPPA.
